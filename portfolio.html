1. Thefaces

The Faces is hiring mobile and web app which easily helps to manage your jobs, candidates, and hiring team. Its a Mobile Recruiting app for the Modern Workforce featuring native apps for both iOS and Android, and mobile-friendly, responsive design.

The app allows job seekers and employer to share candidate's profile outside the application as well. Employers can shortlist and hire suitable candidates, and rejects candidates that don’t fit too. In today's fast paced business environment, managers and executives need to have all their resources at their fingertips anytime, anywhere. Companies who have not adopted an efficient mobile strategy are behind in the digital race and are losing out to competitors who have embraced mobility, therefore gaining access to more candidates.

Registration is free for both recruiters and job seekers and registered email verification is mandatory before further access to application. The location of registered recruiters and job seekers are detected automatically by the application once logged in. The app is a real time location tracking application which displays registered job seekers and posted jobs in a location map which eases Hiring managers to easily connect with candidates from anywhere in the world and job seekers to find posted jobs available anywhere in the world. Thus app attracts audience from different part of the world. It primarily displays jobs and job seekers available within your location for faster search. TheFaces app has a unique feature to upload job seeker video resume during registration which is one of easiest way to impress recruiters. Similarly recruiters are allowed to upload video about the company; this will help faster approval of recruiting company registration by the company admin. The app has you complete a profile on the mobile or web and upload a resume and video resume, which you can edit as you go along, to customize to the needs of particular employers. You're able to bookmark a job that interests you as favourite, and then apply for it later once you're on your home computer. After you set up a profile, you type in the kind of job you're looking and your location to get rolling. The app's search filter scans through millions of listings on the site to find potential jobs for you based on location, salary, and job title. With TheFaces mobile app and user-friendly platform, Employers will be able to decentralize recruitment. This will help hiring team to be more involved and be very hands on. Thus app helps Employers across the world to Hire Top Talents globally.
