Kibarona

Alzheimer's disease is a type of dementia that affects memory, cognition, and behavior. Unlike with any other disease, Alzheimer's patient rely heavily on their family to get by on a daily basis. It is critical to provide excellent care to these patients while also keeping them safe and secure . Care givers' lack of knowledge about the disease usually worsens their patients' conditions.

To provide all love and support to our elders, we launch a new iniative - KIBARONA. Kibarona is a unique application developed with the UAE Vision. It is an application designed for Alzheimer's patients.

The application is intended to improve the patients' condition. The app includes several memory-boosting games as well as other materials that can improve the patient's condition. The app also provides expert knowledge to caregivers through a 40-hour course. The course materials are prepared by highly qualified and experienced doctors. The app can communicate with IoT devices to ensure patient safety. Device like wearables, infrared camera can notify the patient's movement to take necessary measures for their safety

Caregiver receives certificate once course is completed and test is passed.
